About Us

San Diego Chapter of the Antique Airplane Association

Meetings are generally held on the first Tuesday of each month at 6:00 PM at the Montgomery Field Terminal Building ground floor conference room (Casa Machado Restaurant is upstairs). Fly-outs, tours, picnics and chapter updates are announced via member email and website updates. Membership is open to anyone interested in historic aircraft; a pilot license or aircraft ownership is not required.

AAASDC Contact

  • 1872 Joe Crosson Dr #103, El Cajon, CA 92020
  • 619-548-0435
  • sdchapteraaa@gmail.com

Membership Information

Annual membership is listed as $30 for check/cash payments and $31 for online credit-card payments. Voting members and officers are also expected to maintain national Antique Airplane Association membership.
